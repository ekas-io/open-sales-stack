# Example: Account with Inconclusive Signals

This example demonstrates how to handle situations where some signals cannot be evaluated due to crawl failures or missing data. Notice the distinction between "crawl failed" and "data not found."

---

# NovaPay Solutions

**Website:** https://novapay.io
**LinkedIn:** https://linkedin.com/company/novapay-solutions

## Summary
NovaPay Solutions is a fintech company providing payment processing and invoicing tools for small businesses. Founded in 2019, headquartered in San Francisco, CA with approximately 85 employees.

## Qualification Verdict: QUALIFIED

| # | Signal | Result |
|---|--------|--------|
| 1 | Website (free trial / self-serve signup / product launch) | Inconclusive |
| 2 | SDR Job — Inbound Lead Handling | Inconclusive |
| 3 | LinkedIn Ads (>1 active) | Match |
| 4 | Recent Funding (<6 months) | Match |
| 5 | Product Launch / Press Release | Inconclusive |

**Signals Matched:** 2 of 5
**Qualifying Signals:** LinkedIn Ads, Recent Funding
